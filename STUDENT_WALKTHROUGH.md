# Campus Flow - Student Walkthrough

Welcome to **Campus Flow**! This guide will show you how to get the most out of your new academic resource platform.

## 1. The Dashboard: Your HQ
Upon logging in, you land on your **Smart Dashboard**.
- **Stats at a Glance**: Check your Coins, XP, and current league ranking.
- **Daily Missions**: Complete these small tasks (e.g., "Read 1 Note") to earn rewards.
- **Recent Activity**: Quickly jump back into resources you were viewing.

## 2. Resource Hub: Find What You Need
Navigate to the **Resources** tab to access the library.
- **Smart Filters**: Select your *Course*, *Branch*, and *Year* to see only relevant materials.
- **Preview**: Click any resource to preview it instantly without downloading.
- **Interact**: 'Like' useful notes to save them for later or help others find them.

## 3. Flashcards: Active Recall
Go to the **Study** section and choose **Flashcards**.
- **Pick a Deck**: Choose a topic you need to revise.
- **Test Yourself**: Flip cards and rate your confidence ("Hard", "Medium", "Easy").
- **Track Progress**: The system remembers your weak spots and helps you focus on them.

## 4. Study Rooms: Focus Together
Need motivation? Join a **Virtual Study Room**.
- **Pomodoro Timer**: Use the built-in timer to work in focused bursts (e.g., 25 mins work, 5 mins break).
- **Live Chat**: Quietly chat with peers in the room to share goals or ask quick questions.

## 5. Community Forum: Ask & Answer
Stuck on a concept? Head to the **Forum**.
- **Ask**: Post a specific question.
- **Answer**: Help others and earn extra XP!
- **Search**: Chances are, someone else has already asked your question.

---
**Tip**: Keep an eye on the Leaderboard! The top students every week earn special badges.
